About Me

Hello! I've spent nearly a decade in Japan, starting in 2014 as a MEXT scholar in Aizawa Lab where I completed my Ph.D. in 2018. I then worked at NTT Communication Science Laboratories and joined Hitachi, Ltd. in 2021. Currently, I'm on secondment with Astemo, continuing my journey in research and innovation.
