About Me

Hello! My journey in Japan began in 2014 as a MEXT scholar in Aizawa Lab. After completing my Ph.D. in 2018, I joined NTT Communication Science Laboratories. Since 2021, I've been with Hitachi, Ltd. and currently, I'm on secondment with Astemo, embracing new challenges and innovations.
